The Zuumer

GreenPedals is now an authorized dealer for ZuumCraft. We chose the Zuumer due to its unique three wheel design which allows for maximum stability while leaning hard into tight turns. This thrilling new concept scooter is great for either commuting or recreational riding. Additionally, we expect a market for Zuumers from official organizations such as police departments and parks management.
