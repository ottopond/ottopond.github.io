This summer Boris Mordkovish and Anna
Mostovetsky will ride electric bikes from New York
City to San Francisco to draw attention to the
practicality of green alternative transportation.

GreenPedals will host an event to hear them speak along with other green transportation advocates, Iain Banksand Eric Fingerhut. Join us for hors' d'oeuvres and drinks. Also, check out and test ride out menagerie of light electric vehicles.





April 11th, 6:00 - 9:00

413-280-5005

105 B Annapolis St,

Annapolis, MD 21401




Iain Banks serves as the Personal Transportation and
Parking Specialist for the city of Annapolis. Along with
developing a bicycling master plan his responsibilities
include building partnerships with bicycle and pedestrian
groups and promoting safety and education programs to
enhance bicycling.

Eric Fingerhut is a lawyer and member
of LEVA's (Light Electric Vehicle
Association) legislative comittee. He
will briefly discuss legal issues related
to the iuse of e-bikes and speak on the
European including electric bikes, socooters and electric bike boom as he witnessed on his recent tour of London, Copenhagen and Zurich.
