  • Frame: 6061 Aluminium Alloy T4 T6
  • Tyres: 26" Schwalbe Marathon Plus with Kevlar guard & relfective band
  • Rim: Weinmann, Al Alloy double wall, CNC sidewall
  • Spokes: 13G Stainless steel
  • Brakes (Front): Shimano hydraulic disc brakes
  • Brakes (Rear): Shimano hydraulic disc brakes
  • Gears: Shimano DEORE 8 speeds
  • Front fork: Suspension front fork with Al Alloy crown 50mm travel
  • Handlebars: Al Alloy
  • Chainguard: Polycarbonate
  • Mudguard: Polycarbonate with stainless steel supports
  • Chainwheel: Al Alloy chainring and crank, 48T
  • Chain: KMD Rust resistant
  • Stand: Steel central support stand
  • Rear Carrier: Stainless steel up to 40 kgs

  • Lighting: Super White LED front and red LED rear, powered by main battery
  • Electric System
  • Motor- Nominal rated 350W. Brushless DC hub motor with planetary gears. Peak wattage up to 700W
  • Controller- PWM microprocessor and LED self diagnostic system. Overload protection 20 Amps, Underv voltage protection 31.5 V. E-Bike, Pedal Assist, Dual Mode options available
  • Battery- 37V 10Ah Lithium Ion Polymer battery 3.1 kgs
  • Charger- 2 amps smart charger. Maximum charge time 5.5 hours
  • Performance- Maximum Speed 20mph
  • Range- ~ 45 kms (28 miles) on Ebike mode, 40 ~ 60 kms on Pedal Assist Mode
  • Bike weight- 50 lbs
